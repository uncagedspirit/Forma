# Privacy Policy for Forma

Last updated: May 18, 2026

## Introduction

Forma ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Forma habit tracker mobile application ("App").

## Data Collection Practices

Forma is designed with privacy as a core principle. **We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.**

The only data the App processes is:
- Habit names and tracking logs you create
- Mood entries and notes you log
- Goal names and categories you set
- App preferences (theme, notification settings, etc.)
- Timestamps of habit completions

## Local Storage (Hive)

All your data is stored **locally on your device** using the Hive database engine. This means:
- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it
- We have no access to your habits, moods, goals, or any other information you enter
- Deleting the App will delete all locally stored data unless you have created a backup

## No Third-Party Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud synchronization services that would access your personal information.

## Export Feature

The App provides an export feature that allows you to save your data to a file on your device. When you use this feature:
- The exported file is created locally on your device
- You are solely responsible for any copies you create or share
- We do not receive or process exported data

## User Rights

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:
- **Access**: View all your data within the App at any time
- **Modification**: Edit or update any habit, goal, mood entry, or preference
- **Deletion**: Delete individual items or clear all data through the App settings
- **Portability**: Export your data using the built-in export feature

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, the App uses your device's local notification system to schedule reminders. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Forma is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and updated with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
