Forma helps you build better habits through simple daily tracking.

Features:
• Track unlimited habits with beautiful visualizations
• Monitor your mood and see correlations with habits
• Set goals and organize habits by category
• View detailed statistics and insights
• Maintain streaks and stay motivated
• Export your data anytime

Forma is designed to be calm, focused, and delightful to use.
No overwhelming dashboards — just your habits, your progress, and your growth.

Privacy-first: All your data stays on your device.
